My Career Aspirations

My career aspiration is to pursue research-oriented graduate study and contribute to the development of efficient, reliable, and responsible machine learning systems.

Research-Oriented Graduate Study

I aim to continue my academic journey through graduate-level research in computer science, with a focus on machine learning and large language model systems. I am particularly interested in how models can be trained and adapted efficiently under practical constraints such as limited resources, privacy requirements, and deployment feasibility.

Through graduate study, I hope to strengthen my ability to design sound experiments, analyze results critically, and communicate research findings clearly in written and visual forms.

Long-Term Research Direction

In the long term, I aspire to work in research-driven environments where I can explore the intersection of theory and practice. My goal is to contribute to research that not only proposes new ideas, but also validates them through careful evaluation and reproducible implementation.

By continuously improving my technical foundation and research skills, I hope to participate in projects that advance efficient and trustworthy AI systems with real-world impact.
