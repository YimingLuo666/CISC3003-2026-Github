My Goals and Development

My Goals and Intended Learning Outcomes

My goal is to develop into a well-prepared Computer Science student with strong academic foundations, practical technical ability, and clear research interests. Through my studies and project work, I aim to strengthen my understanding of programming, web development, artificial intelligence, and large language models. I also want to improve my ability to analyze problems carefully, communicate technical ideas clearly, and produce structured, professional-quality work that is suitable for both academic and real-world settings.

My Personal / Professional Development Planning

In my personal and professional development planning, I focus on continuous growth through coursework, research, internships, and project-based learning. Academically, I plan to deepen my knowledge in AI-related areas, especially efficient and practical intelligent systems. Professionally, I want to keep improving my technical skills in programming, web design, system development, and documentation, so that I can contribute effectively in both graduate study and future technical roles. I also aim to build a strong portfolio that presents my qualifications, achievements, and readiness for future opportunities.

My Reflection and Future Directions

Looking back, I have grown through a combination of academic study, research exposure, web programming practice, and technical project experience. These experiences have helped me become more disciplined, reflective, and confident in presenting my ideas. In the future, I hope to continue this development by pursuing advanced study and taking part in more meaningful technical and research projects. My long-term direction is to work on useful, efficient, and well-designed digital systems, especially in areas related to intelligent applications and deployable AI.
